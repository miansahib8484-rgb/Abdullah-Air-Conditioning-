Who We Are

شركة عبدالله لتكييف الهواء — الرياض

Abdullah Air Conditioning is a fully registered, VAT-compliant Saudi company based in حي القدس, Riyadh. Founded in 2009 by Abdullah Al-Qahtani — a licensed HVAC engineer — the company was built on a single principle: give Riyadh residents honest, expert appliance service they can count on without worry.

We specialize in air conditioning installation and repair, washing machine repair, refrigerator repair, and full commercial HVAC solutions. With 35+ SASO-certified technicians stationed across Riyadh, we serve thousands of homes, villas, compounds, offices and government buildings every year.

We are a 100% Saudi-owned and operated business, proud contributors to Vision 2030, committed to quality local employment and world-class technical standards across every district of Riyadh.
